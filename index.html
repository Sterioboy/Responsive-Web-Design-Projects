Donatello

[The best ninja ever existed]

Survey Form

His primary signature weapon are his effective bō staffs. In all media, he is the smartest of the four turtles.

The 1987 animated television series

Donatello is the genius of the group who invents many of the turtles vehicles and equipment including the Turtle Van, the Turtle Blimp and the Turtle Com. He’s the calmer turtle out of the 4 turtles. Donatello made many revolutionary inventions, the most notable being the portable portal capable of opening gateways to other dimensions as well as an early warning system which warns of impending attacks from other dimensions or from Krang and Shredder.
